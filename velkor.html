The Velkor Fragrance Guide

Understanding Notes (The Pyramid)

Fragrances evolve over time, much like a pyramid of scent. The **Top** notes are what you smell first (usually fresh and light); the **Heart** notes form the core identity (floral/spicy); and the **Base** notes provide depth and longevity (wood/musk).

How To Apply for Longevity

To make your Velkor scent last all day, apply it to warm **pulse points**‚Äîwrists, neck, and behind the ears. For an extra boost, apply to moisturized skin. Do not rub the perfume, as this breaks down the molecules.
